The Beginning

The Day Everything Changed

That moment—the first time I saw you—was the most special moment of my life. I first spotted you walking through the park, and—you probably won't believe this—I developed a crush on you the instant I saw you. I loved your cute, innocent face, and—what I loved most of all—the way you walked; honestly, I still love that. You know, before going to the park, I used to pray to God that I’d run into you—and that you’d be alone—so I could talk to you and tell you how I felt. But I never could, because back then, so many of my friends liked you too—I don't know... they were better-looking, wealthier, and more fit than me, so I honestly never gathered the courage to tell you. But everything changed the day I went to tuition and saw you for the first time; I felt so happy then. You know, you ended up sitting right next to me, and my heart started pounding hard. It used to make me so angry when Harshit and the other kids asked you for your water bottle—and you’d actually give it to them😡. I’d get the most annoyed with *you* for that. To be honest, my romantic interest in you had actually faded a bit back then, even though my heart felt something else entirely; that’s why I just wanted to be friends. But when we started chatting on Instagram, it felt amazing—I used to pray to God for you to come into my life, and He finally answered that prayer after two years. Still, I’m a bit angry with God; I feel I should have entered your life back in the 9th grade—before Shivam came along. But now I think that whatever God did was for the best; if I had entered your life back then, you probably wouldn't love me as much as you do now, nor would you have changed yourself for me. You might have just considered me a way to pass the time.

When Things Got Hard

Misunderstandings. Silence. Distance. Family pressure. We faced more in one year than most couples do in five. There were days I didn't know if we'd make it. But we did — because what we have is real.The real troubles in our lives began when I came to meet you after your Home Science exam and your uncle saw us—that is the biggest regret of my life. One Year

Here We Are

365 days of love, growth, pain, laughter, tears, and everything in between. I wouldn't trade a single difficult day, because every one of them led us here — stronger, deeper, and more in love than ever.

💌

In this love letter, I want to tell you all over again that, even looking ahead to the coming year, I still want to make you my life ,

dear akriti nayak

I've written this a thousand times in my head and deleted it every time, because no words ever felt enough. But today, on the day that marks one whole year of us, I have to try.

This year has not been easy. You know that better than anyone. There were nights when the silence between us felt unbearable. Times when circumstances, misunderstandings, and the weight of the world around us made everything feel impossible. I watched you carry burdens that weren't yours to carry alone, and I wish — I truly wish — I could have done more.

But even in the hardest moments, even in the silences I didn't understand and the distances I couldn't cross, my feelings never once wavered. I want you to know that. What I feel for you isn't conditional on perfect circumstances or easy days. It just is — steady, certain, and entirely yours.

I see you. I see how strong you are. I see the battles you fight that no one else knows about. I see the sacrifices you make for the people you love. And I need you to know: none of that has ever made me love you less. It has only made me love you more.

"Mohabbat woh nahi jo sirf khushiyon mein saath de, Mohabbat toh woh hai jo tootne ke hazaar wajah hone ke baad bhi saath rehne ki ek wajah dhoond le."
Every memory with you has become a part of me. Your smile became my favorite place, your happiness became my favorite wish, and your presence became my favorite feeling. Even on the days when we couldn't understand each other, my heart never forgot how deeply it loved you.
"Log kehte hain waqt sab kuch badal deta hai, Par kuch ehsaas aise hote hain jo waqt ke saath aur gehre ho jaate hain."
So today, on our first anniversary, I want to ask you something from the deepest corner of my heart. Just like I asked for your love once before, I am asking again.
Will you choose me again?
Will you stay beside me through every sunrise and every storm?
Will you let me spend not just another year, but a lifetime loving you?
Will you continue being my Akriti today, tomorrow, and forever?
Because if I had to choose a thousand times, in a thousand different lives, I would still choose you.
"Main tumse sirf aaj ke liye pyaar nahi karta, Main tumse un sab kal ke liye pyaar karta hoon jo abhi aaye hi nahi."

Thank you for still being here. Thank you for not giving up on us, even when it would have been easier to. Thank you for letting me love you imperfectly, messily, completely.

You are not just my person. You are the reason I believe that love is worth all the hard parts.

Forever and always,

Your someone 💕
